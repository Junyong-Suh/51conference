Why attend?

Real talk with the insiders.

The goal of the 51 Conference is to offer community-driven network to support entrepreneurs and tech professionals across the world. We bring together some of the Silicon Valley’s outstanding innovators to share the latest industry trends and know-hows of life in tech. The 3- day event features 30+ compelling keynotes, panels, and workshops to cultivate critical-thinking skills and promote disruptive ideas.

  • Latest industry trends and new opportunities in tech
  • Taste of Silicon Valley’s different startup cultures
  • Access to industry experts to expand your network
  • Professional advices from great minds in tech
  • Personal help on improving your resume
