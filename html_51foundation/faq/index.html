F.A.Q

For attendees

Why the conference?

Our mission is to educate youth from Korea about opportunities and emerging technologies in Silicon Valley by connecting them with industry leaders and future mentors.

Who is the conference for?

51Conference is designed for Korean students and professionals interested in careers and opportunities in Silicon Valley.

When and where will it be?

51Conference 2016 will take place July 28-30 at the Mission Bay Conference Center in San Francisco.

What is the conference schedule?

Visit our Conference Schedule page for details.

How can I register?

Registration is now open! Register online here.

What are the registration rates?

Registration rate for 51Conference is US$850.

Can I register a group?

Yes. Please contact info@51conference.org for assistance.

What’s included in the cost of the conference?

The registration fee covers 2½ days of conference, give-aways, and meals.

Are all meals provided?

All meals during the time of the conference will be provided.

  • Thursday: breakfast, boxed lunch, and dinner
  • Friday: breakfast, boxed lunch, and dinner
  • Saturday: lunch

What is the cancellation policy for the conference?

Registration fees are refundable, less a $35 processing fee, up until July 15. No refund will be issued for cancellations starting July 16, 2016.

How can my organization become a conference sponsor?

To become a sponsor and for more information, please contact info@51conference.org

My question is not listed here. Whom do I contact?

Please contact info@51conference.org for assistance.

For presenters

Should I present in English or Korean?

Korean is preferred, but either language is acceptable.

Is parking available?

Parking is available in the conference building, with fees by the hour.

What audio/visual equipment is available?

There is a microphone and a projector in each presenting area.
