For attendees Why the conference? Our mission is to educate youth from Korea about opportunities and emerging technologies in Silicon Valley by connecting them with industry leaders and future mentors. Who is the conference for? 51Conference is designed for Korean students and professionals interested in careers and opportunities in Silicon Valley. Can I register a group? Yes. Please contact info@51conference.org for assistance. What’s included in the cost of the conference? The registration fee covers 2½ days of conference, give-aways, and meals. Are all meals provided? All meals during the time of the conference will be provided. - Thursday: breakfast, boxed lunch, and dinner - Friday: breakfast, boxed lunch, and dinner - Saturday: lunch What is the cancellation policy for the conference?
